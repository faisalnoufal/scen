Foritigate Firewall Training (NSE-4)

Fortinet is an American MNC headquartered in Sunnyvale, California. It is into Cyber Security. It not only develops but also markets Cyber security software and appliances like Firewalls, anti-viruses etc. It is 4th largest network security in the world on the basis of revenue.

FortiGate Firewall is one of the products of Fortinet. It provides a consolidated security platform to deliver unmatched performance and protection to the network thereby simplifying it as well. Fortinet has provided with models to satisfy the requirements of small offices through FortiGate-30 series and FortiGate-5000 series for very large enterprises. To provide a comprehensive and high performance security, FortiGate platform combines FortiOS™ security operating system with FortiASIC processors
