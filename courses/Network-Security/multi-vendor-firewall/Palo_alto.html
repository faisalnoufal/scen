Palo Alto Networks certification offers a mastery over wide set of enterprise-level next-generation firewalls, with a varied range of security features for your network. Palo Alto Networks next-generation firewalls are designed, architected and implemented to enable applications safely and prevent them from modern threats. Our Palo Alto training course allows participants to get trained and equip themselves with latest skills and tools necessary to gain expertise on next generation firewalls. They learn to configure and manage firewalls, Mitigate threats with Traps and Wild Fire, manage multiple firewalls with Panorama as well and much more. The course has been designed in a manner to help engineers in getting acquainted with the skills required to understand, support, troubleshoot and manage Palo Alto Networks Next-Generation Security Platform products in their care.

The Palo Alto Networks certification through instructor-led training at SCEN cyber lab enables you to::

  • Configure & Manage the essential features of Palo Alto Networks next-generation firewalls.
  • Configure & Manage Global Protect to protect systems, located outside the data center perimeter.
  • Monitor network traffic with the help of interactive web interface and firewall reports.
  • Get Assistance with training lab set up:
    • Using our fully bundled VM-100 virtual machine appliances
    • Using the NDG Netlab+ services to run Palo Alto Networks labs
