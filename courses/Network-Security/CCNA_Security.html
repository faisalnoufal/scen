Implementing Cisco Network Security Exam (210-260)

Course Description

There is a huge gap of Security professionals on the market, and this can be your starting point in the Security world, a complex but fun and challenging track. By becoming CCNA Security certified, a network professional demonstrates the skills required to develop a security infrastructure, recognise threats and vulnerabilities to networks, and mitigate security threats. This course will help candidates obtain a deeper understanding of the technologies from the blueprint, which will tremendously help to prepare for the certification exam and also for real-life deployments. Lab topologies will be designed, explaining the objective for each design, and then built from scratch by the instructor, nothing being pre-scripted, pre-tested or pre-recorded . During the process, the instructor will intentionally demonstrate the effects of misconfigurations and failures, randomly making learners think how to approach a specific problem, never seen before. You will also learn how to build a proper study and learning methodology.

The CCNA Security Exam Prep course will help candidates who are nearing their CCNA lab dates to refine and consolidate their technical knowledge into an organised and effective strategy for passing their lab exams. It serves as the perfect last review step before sitting for the exam.
