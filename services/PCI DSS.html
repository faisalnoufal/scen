PCI DSS

The Payment Card Industry Security Standards Council has developed a number of robust and comprehensive standards and supporting materials to enhance payment card data security.

These materials include a framework of specifications, tools, measurements and supporting resources to help organisations secure the way in which cardholder data is processed.

The Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard (PCI DSS) is the primary standard that provides a security framework that requires an organisation to meet the relevant sections dependent on the scope of the organisation and IT environment

PCI Managed Service

This service allows organisations to focus on business delivery and allow PCI requirements to be managed by us as a trusted partner of their organisation.

  • In the Assessment and Planning phases of our methodology we identify all of a company’s responsibilities under the standard.
  • We then conduct analysis on the requirement the environment in scope and produce our PCI Compliance Matrix.  This ensure all key compliance standards are delivered throughout the compliance calendar year and that your PCI compliance remains seamless.
  • Documentation is managed via our secure work-space that uses government certified encryption so you can rest assured your data is in safe hands. Certified Qualified Security Assessor (QSA)

Qualified Security Assessors are individuals that have been certified by the Council to validate an entity’s adherence to the PCI DSS requirements. They are subjected to rigorous assessment in order to become a QSA and must meet the requirements of the PCI Security Standards Council.

By working with a QSA organisations can ensure they are on the right track throughout their PCI DSS Journey. This support ranges from design assessment, scope verification or supporting the completion of all key documentation required for PCI DSS Certification.
